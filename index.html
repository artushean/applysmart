Listing Engagement Risk Analyzer

A decision-support tool for job seekers to estimate how much effort to invest in an application.

Important Disclaimer

This tool estimates listing engagement risk based on posting patterns and language structure. It does not determine employer intent and does not label listings as fake or ghost jobs.

To reduce legal and privacy risk, this MVP does not collect company names, does not scrape job platforms, and relies only on user-provided listing details.
